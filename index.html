About Me

Welcome to my digital nest, where creativity and coding intertwine! Here, I harness a magpie's zest for discovery and a raven's keen intellect to bring projects to life.

I've been weaving websites and cawing with code since my teenage years in the 90s, but now I'm spreading my wings and taking it much more seriously.

My mission is to craft innovative and fun solutions that resonate with depth and meaning, whether they flutter as personal expressions or take flight as professional endeavors. As I nurture these projects, I find joy in watching them grow, much like a mother bird tending to her eggs. I’m excited for the ideas I’m incubating to hatch and soar into the world, each one ready to explore its own horizon.
