pndnrecords.com

pndnrecords.com is a website that chronicles the story of the punk music label I founded, which operated from 2000 to 2003 in Portland, Oregon. This project captures the essence of the DIY ethos that defined our approach to music promotion, highlighting the bands we supported and the cultural impact we made during our brief existence.

Key Features:

  • A detailed narrative of the label's history, mission, and the punk scene during the early 2000s.
  • An overview of the bands we worked with, emphasizing the community spirit.
  • Built using HTML and CSS, featuring a clean design that reflects our punk roots.

Despite its short run, PNDN Records left a lasting mark on the Portland punk landscape, showcasing the power of grassroots movements in shaping cultural narratives.

Technologies Used
